Other Insecure Options¶

There are other insecure options that you may find Apache supports.

It’s left to the reader to try those out. Open OnDemand developers highly discourage users attempting to authenticate with Apache’s BASIC auth like PAM and LDAP as they are really quite insecure.

Questions on these topics will be linked back to this page.
