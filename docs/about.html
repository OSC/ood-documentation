What is Open OnDemand¶

The Open OnDemand Project [1] is an NSF-funded [2] open-source HPC portal based on the Ohio Supercomputer Center’s original OnDemand portal. The goal of Open OnDemand is to provide an easy way for system administrators to provide web access to their HPC resouces.
